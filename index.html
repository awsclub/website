AWS Cloud Club at CCT College Dublin

A free student led community supported by Amazon Web Services. Open to everyone 18+ in Ireland. Join us online or on campus to learn, build and grow together.

About Us

We help students and professionals master cloud computing through workshops, projects and peer learning. Based at CCT College Dublin, our activities are free and open to all.

Our Vision

We believe cloud skills should be accessible to all. Our goal is to connect, inspire, and prepare members for the opportunities of a cloud-first job market.

Our Team

Led by student ambassadors and volunteers, the club grows with every member. Your ideas and energy shape what we do.
