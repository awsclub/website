AWS Cloud Club at CCT College Dublin

A free student-led community supported by Amazon Web Services. Open to everyone 18+ in Ireland. Join us online or on campus to learn, build and grow together.

About Us

Help students and professionals master cloud computing through workshops, projects and peer learning. Base activities at CCT College Dublin. Keep participation free and open to all.

Our Vision

Ensure cloud skills remain accessible to all. Connect, inspire, and prepare members for a cloud-first job market.

Our Team

Lead the club with ambassadors and volunteers. Encourage members to shape direction with ideas and energy.
